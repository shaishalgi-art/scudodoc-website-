Architecture: Hybrid Flask App

One rich scrolling homepage (index.html) tells the full investor/product story. Four dedicated sub-pages give depth to Technology, ESG, Market, and Team — linked from the nav. All rendered server-side with Flask + Jinja2. TailwindCSS via CDN (no build step).

Homepage (index.html)
  • Confidential banner
  • Sticky nav + mobile menu
  • Hero + product render + stats
  • Problem (6 cards)
  • Solution overview
  • ESG Impact summary
  • Competitive positioning
  • Market overview
  • Team snippets
  • Investment CTA
  • Footer
Sub-pages
  • /technology — Ion Plasma deep-dive, science table, NAI explanation, video
  • /esg — Full ESG stats, environmental crisis, ECO-SCUDO program
  • /market — Market phases, revenue model, projections table
  • /team — Full team profiles + investment section
  • /contact — Contact form (mailto)

The Problem

Why 100-Year-Old Technology Still Dominates

😮‍💨

Fatigue & Discomfort

Heat and moisture buildup cause exhaustion, reducing clinical concentration and patient care quality.

🗣️

Poor Patient Interaction

Masks obstruct vocal clarity and facial visibility, degrading doctor-patient interaction quality.

⚠️

Mediocre Protection

N95: only 60% actual on-face filtering. KN95: 48.3%. Surgical: as low as 10–15% — despite 95%+ lab certification.

💶

High Ongoing Costs

Private GP/dental clinics spend €500–€1,000/year on over 2,000 disposable masks per doctor-assistant pair.

🌍

Massive ESG Failure

~4 billion masks/year from GP & dental clinics alone — 100% non-recyclable, classified hazardous (DASRI), persisting 450+ years as microplastics.

🚢

Supply Chain Vulnerability

EU imports 70%, USA 90% of medical masks from China. Geopolitical risk, tariffs (US 25–54%), and EU Reg. 2025/1197 make this dependency untenable.
